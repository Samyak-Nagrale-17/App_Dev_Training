Meet your Instructors

Preeti Karn

A master of traditional Indian arts, Preeti specializes in Warli and Kalamkari painting, bringing their rich cultural heritage to life through intricate designs and storytelling.

Tyeb Mehta

Known for his unique interpretation of Indian folk art, Tyeb Mehta will guide you through the vibrant world of Madhubani painting, renowned for its vivid colors and mythological themes.

Jatin Das

A celebrated artist with a deep connection to Gond painting, Jatin brings this tribal art form's folklore and symbolism to light, using bold patterns and earthy tones.
